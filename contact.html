DJ Esteban

I want to model my website after the music producer Alesso (see image attached above). The reason for this is because it is a very simplistic design, and his colors are very minimal as well. If I ever get personalized merchandise, I also want to use his website as a guide.

Estimated pages: 3

Page #1: Live mixes

My hope with this page is to provide the audience some examples of live mixes I have done in the past. For example, one video could show a live hip-hop mix and a second video with an EDM mix.

Page #2: Mashups

This page will show the audience some of the mashups of songs I have done in the past. My aim is demonstrate my ability to mix 2 or more songs together.

Page #3: Prices

The purpose of this page is to inform my audience of the prices I charge based off of the amount of hours I am asked to play. The prices are still to be decided.

Goals for this website

1. Advertise myself in order to grow as a musician

2. Introduce people to the world of DJing and Electronic Dance Music (EDM)

3. Use this website in order to be hired for gigs

Target Audience

My target audience is anyone who wants to hire a DJ for any sort of event, people interested in learning morea about EDM music, and current people that enjoy EDM music. The age range could vary; they could be as young as 9 years old to 40 years old.
